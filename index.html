About Us

Pathways to Autism Care is dedicated to connecting families with trusted, experienced caregivers who provide specialized care for children on the autism spectrum. Our goal is to simplify the process of finding the right caregiver for your child's unique needs.

We do not act as an employer. Instead, we help families and caregivers connect directly, allowing them to negotiate terms that work for both parties. With Pathways to Autism Care, you have the support you need to find the best match for your family.
